### THE HISTORICAL CONTROL GROUP

I want you to travel with me in the other direction. Not to the future. To the past.

<div class="archive-quote">
  <strong>79 AD | POMPEII:</strong> People huddled in groups, clutching household figurines of their gods as Vesuvius erupted. Vesuvius was unimpressed.
  <br/><br/>
  <strong>1349 | BLACK DEATH:</strong> Flagellant processions moved across Europe, whipping themselves for mercy. The plague killed one-third of the continent regardless.
  <br/><br/>
  <strong>1918 | SPANISH FLU:</strong> 100 million dead. The virus moved according to replication dynamics that had zero relationship to the spiritual status of its hosts.
</div>

<p class="impact-text purple">The universe's physics are not subject to petition.</p>

Prayer is a tool for the interior. It shapes the self. It cannot shape the trajectory of an 800-metre asteroid. It cannot change the replication rate of a novel pathogen.
