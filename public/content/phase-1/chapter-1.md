### THE KITTY HAWK ANOMALY

**December 17, 1903 | 10:35 AM**

At Kill Devil Hills, North Carolina, Orville Wright became the first human being in history to achieve sustained, controlled, powered flight.

### THE TOOLS OF THE GODS

Think about what changed between 1903 and 1969. The tools — the materials, the mathematics, the computational capacity — improved by a factor that makes comparison almost meaningless.

<div class="warning-block">
  <strong>DATA COMPARISON:</strong><br/>
  An Apollo guidance computer had 4,096 words of RAM. Your phone has more computing power than the entire NASA mission control complex of 1969.
</div>

When the tools improve, the impossible evaporates faster than anyone predicted. This is not optimism. It is data.

---

<div class="gut-punch">
  Capability is not destiny.
</div>

Not one gram of lift at Kitty Hawk came from prayer. Not one metre of altitude over the Sea of Tranquility was achieved by faith. The Moon landing was the product of **Mathematics**, **Chemistry**, and **Physics**.

The moment any one of those problems — re-entry heating, oxygen supply, guidance computers — had been answered with "God will provide," Armstrong would have died.

<p class="impact-text purple">The mind is the most sacred thing in the universe.</p>

It carried us to the Moon. It, and only it, will carry us to the stars.
